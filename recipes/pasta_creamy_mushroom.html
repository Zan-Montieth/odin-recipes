Creamy Mushroom Pasta

Description

The Creamy Mushroom Pasta is a savory and indulgent dish that features al dente pasta coated in a rich and creamy mushroom sauce. The mushrooms add a deep umami flavor and a meaty texture to the dish, while the garlic gives it a subtle kick of heat. The heavy cream and Parmesan cheese combine to create a luscious and velvety sauce that complements the pasta perfectly. The dish is finished off with a sprinkle of fresh parsley, which adds a pop of color and freshness to the plate. This recipe is perfect for a cozy night in or a dinner party, as it is quick and easy to prepare but also tastes and looks impressive.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. pasta
  • 2 tbsp. butter
  • 1 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 lb. sliced mushrooms
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the butter and olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and start to brown, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add the garlic to the skillet and cook for an additional minute.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and add the heavy cream to the skillet. Stir to combine.
  5. Add the grated Parmesan cheese to the skillet and stir until it has melted and the sauce is smooth.
  6. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Drain the pasta and add it to the skillet with the mushroom sauce. Toss to coat the pasta with the sauce.
  8. Serve the pasta hot, garnished with chopped parsley. Enjoy!
