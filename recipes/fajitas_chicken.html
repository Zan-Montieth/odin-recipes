Chicken Fajitas

Description

Chicken Fajitas is a classic Tex-Mex dish that is bursting with flavor and color. Thinly sliced chicken is seasoned with a blend of chili powder, cumin, and garlic powder, and then pan-fried until golden brown. Sliced bell peppers and onions are added to the pan, which add sweetness and crunch to the dish. The fajitas are then served on warm flour tortillas, which provide a soft and pliable base for the filling. Toppings such as sour cream, shredded cheese, and guacamole add creamy, tangy, and buttery flavors to the dish, making it a crowd-pleaser for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • lb. boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into thin strips
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp. vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp. chili powder
  • 1 tsp. cumin
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Flour tortillas, for serving
  • Sour cream, shredded cheese, and guacamole, for topping (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chicken to the skillet and season with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Cook until the chicken is browned and cooked through, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add the sliced bell peppers and onion to the skillet and cook until they are tender and slightly charred, stirring occasionally.
  4. Warm the tortillas in the microwave or on the stovetop.
  5. Serve the chicken fajita mixture on the warm tortillas, topped with sour cream, shredded cheese, and guacamole if desired.
